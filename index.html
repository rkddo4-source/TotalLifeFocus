What I Do

Total Life Focus assists people with disabilities to get the support that they need.

Support Coordination

Providing reliable, structured, and person-centred support to help you navigate the NDIS with confidence.

Why Work With Me

With hands-on lived experience supporting individuals with brain injury, invisible disability, and complex needs, I bring a genuine understanding of the challenges participants and families face. This allows me to advocate strongly, communicate clearly, and deliver support that is both professional and deeply empathetic.

What Sets Me Apart

About Me

Welcome. My name is Tania.

I have years of experience working as a nurse. This all ended when I had a horrific car accident that left me physically and mentally injured. I still suffer from physical pain to this day.

My brain injury required years of rehabilitation just to regain basic motor functions, which was very, very difficult. I myself have experienced the difficulties of being disabled and understand the situation many others find themselves in.

I will advocate for you, care for you and treat you with the personal care and support that you deserve. This is my calling, my true path in life and I'm proud to admit it.

My Values
Respect | Compassion | Honesty | Advocacy | Empowerment

Feel free to contact me if this is the kind of care you need.
